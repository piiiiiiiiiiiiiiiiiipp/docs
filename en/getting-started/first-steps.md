# First Steps After Login — Cloud Native Solutions

After logging into the console, we recommend completing the following steps to set up your workspace.

---

## Step 1 — Create a Billing Account

Before using paid services you need to create a Billing Account.

→ Full instructions: [Creating a Billing Account](../billing/setup.md)

---

## Step 2 — Invite Your Team

Go to **Settings → Organization** and click **Invite Members**.

Enter the email addresses of your colleagues. Each invited person will receive an invitation email and go through the same registration flow you just completed.

Available roles:

| Role | Permissions |
|---|---|
| **Owner** | Full access to all organization resources and settings. At least one Owner is required. |
| **Administrator** | Manages billing, projects, and member access. |
| **Member** | Access to projects and services as configured by the Administrator. |

![img_18.png](img_18.png)

---

## Step 3 — Explore Services

Once billing is set up, you can start using the platform services:

- Go to **CDN** to set up content delivery
- [Go to **Tencent** to connect your Tencent Cloud account](./tencent-onboarding-en.md)
- Go to **Sangfor** to manage Sangfor HCI resources
- Go to **AWS** (Preview) to explore the AWS integration
